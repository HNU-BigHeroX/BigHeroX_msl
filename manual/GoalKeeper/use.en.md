# Goalkeeper Program Getting Started

> Language: [中文](use.md) | **English**

## Quick Start

* Place the robot inside your own goal, specifically **inside the goal mouth**.
* Turn on the emergency-stop switch and power on the chassis. At this point, the coach station should show that the goalkeeper has connected successfully, and you should be able to command the goalkeeper normally.
* If the goalkeeper cannot be commanded normally, for example if it cannot move out of the goal, power off the chassis, push it slightly farther into the goal, and repeat the steps above.

## Set the Goal

* First place the robot inside your own goal, specifically **inside the goal mouth**.

* After connecting to the coach station, **left-click** your own goal and **right-click** the opponent's goal. The coach station should then show that the goalkeeper is inside your own goal, and the goalkeeper should be able to move normally under command.

  <img src="use.assets/1.png" alt="1" style="zoom:80%;" />

* Then confirm the attack direction on the coach station. If the blue team attacks the red team, that is the default case. If the red team attacks the blue team, click the icon in the upper-right corner of the screen to flip the direction.
